Government Advice to stop the spread of COVID-19

Mainland and Scotland are in lockdown and every citizen must follow the Coronavirus(COVID-19): stay at home guidance! These restrictions will remain in place until at least mid-February. Some islands will remain at Level 3.

PLEASE!
  • wear a face covering
  • wash your hands and clean the surfaces regularly
  • maintain social distancing
  • avoid crowded places except for essential purposes
  • self-isolate and book a test if you have COVID-19 symptoms

COVID-19 Symptoms

On average it takes 5-6 days from when someone is infected with the virus for symptoms to show

Most common symptoms:
  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness
Less common symptoms:
  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivits
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell
Serious symptoms:
  • difficulty breathing
  • chest pain or pressure
  • loss of speech or movement
